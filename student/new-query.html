Guidelines for Submitting Queries

  • Be specific about your issue to help us resolve it faster.
  • Include relevant details such as course codes, transaction references, or error messages.
  • Attach screenshots if applicable to help illustrate the issue.
  • Allow up to 48 hours for a response to your query.
  • For urgent matters requiring immediate attention, please contact the department directly by phone.
